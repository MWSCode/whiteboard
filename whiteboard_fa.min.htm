The calculated values are ideal values for water that is heated by an electric instantaneous water heater.
Heaters that use hydraulic mechanical switches, and real world conditions can cause higher electricity prices!

Electricity Consumption Formula:
kWh consumption = water liters x 1.16 x Temperature / 1000

In summer: 18 C
In winter: 10C
Average: 14C
